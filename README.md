# TonyOS

> A personal operating system for design, product strategy, AI-assisted development, and application delivery.

TonyOS is the central ecosystem for everything Tony builds. It connects projects, reusable front-end foundations, product documentation, architectural decisions, workflows, experiments, and learning into one organized system.

The goal is not merely to store code. TonyOS documents how an idea moves from discovery through design, implementation, testing, release, and iteration.

## Core Philosophy

**Build systems, not isolated projects.**

- Build once and reuse responsibly.
- Document important decisions while they are being made.
- Keep product, design, code, data, and testing connected.
- Make complex engineering approachable to front-end-focused builders.
- Use AI as a visible collaborator, not an uncontrolled code generator.
- Turn completed applications into full portfolio case studies.

## First Application: Bush League

Bush League is a sports organization management game centered on running a living baseball franchise.

The game will combine:

- Fast single-game, multi-game, and key-moment simulation
- Roster, lineup, contract, and staff management
- Deep scouting and uncertain prospect evaluation
- Training programs and long-term player development
- Minor-league progression and call-ups

- Ballpark operations, attendance, events, and facilities
- Franchise finances, fan groups, ownership, city relationships, and legacy

Bush League will serve as the first proving ground for the TonyOS development process and shared front-end ecosystem.
